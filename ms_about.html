About

Hi! My name is Emily Rochester and I’m a Web Design student at Montserrat College of Art. One day when I moved into my dorm, I visited my friend who had just recently moved into a new home nearby. I came to her house and a super crazy vision hit me as soon as I reached her doorstep.
So I also have a psychic part to me where I have incredibly vivid dreams and sometimes images or just vibes. On that day, I had this incredibly negative feeling telling me to “run run run”. I got a vision at the same time of this girl who was about to die once this tall thing fell on her. And then another vision of this TV in the attic. I just had to enter and check out her new house! Usually if I get a bad vibe, I leave. But for this one, I seriously needed to check this place out.
She welcomed me in and we chatted a bit about our lives. She has a baby niece. She was showing me pictures. So. Freaking. Cute. Anyways after a bit of chatting I asked her what was up with this place cause my stomach started hurting and as well as a headache. Probably because of the vibes. It was sickening there. She brought me up to her attic to show me a bunch of items the last owners left behind. It was a lot, and very old, and I was very surprised they left so much behind. But now, I understand why.
This website is dedicated to the items we found in her attic. I would come down to her house after school and on the weekends to work on decoding everything that happened there. I got so immersed in the process…The story is just so impressive! And Alby had taste when it came to art. I’m very impressed by his work.
